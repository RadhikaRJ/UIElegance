Alert

Using the CSS class .alert you can highlight and bring important information or update to notice.

Copy below link into head tag of your HTML file:

.alert

Attention! This is a simple alert! ×

.caution-alert

Proceed with caution! ×

.warning-alert

Warning! You are leaving the cart without checking out! ×

.success-alert

Success! Your payment has been received! ×

.notification-alert

There's an unread email waiting for you! ×
